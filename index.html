Our Story

A Woman-Led Studio Born from Love

Baby Stories by Archa is a women-owned photography boutique nestled in the heart of Kollam, Kerala. Founded with a passion for preserving life's most fleeting and tender moments, we specialize in creating heartwarming portraits of newborns, babies, and families.

Our team is warm, patient, and deeply experienced in working with the tiniest of subjects. We understand that every baby has their own pace — and we let them lead. The result? Authentic, gentle, and breathtakingly beautiful photographs that tell your unique story.
